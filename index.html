  About

For years, I've seen and heard music from underrated and undiscovered artists all around the world.
It's really sad how they make such great music, and yet nobody really recognizes that.
With NV&N, I plan to change that. I'm going to reach out to those artist and offer them a spot on my label.
It might not make much of a difference the first year or two, but after the label grows a little, I'm sure it'll pay off, and the artists will get the credit they really deserve.
This is my plan for NV&N, and I hope it becomes a reality!
